MMMAth — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MMMAth ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect any personal data. We do not require an account, email address, name, or any other personally identifiable information to use MMMAth.

Game progress (completed puzzles, hint usage, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

3. Advertising

MMMAth is free and ad-supported. We use third-party advertising networks to display ads within the App. These ad networks may collect certain information for the purpose of serving relevant advertisements, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • General device information (device model, operating system version)
  • IP address (which may be used to infer approximate location at the city or region level)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, taps)

This data is collected and processed by the ad networks according to their own privacy policies. We do not have access to or control over the personal data collected by these networks.

4. Third-Party Ad Partners

Our ad partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to provide and measure advertisements. You can limit ad tracking through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads

5. Children's Privacy

MMMAth is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The third-party ad networks used in the App are configured to comply with applicable regulations regarding children's privacy.

6. Data Retention

Since MMMAth does not collect personal data, there is no personal data retained on our side. Game progress data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data.

7. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard application storage, which is sandboxed and protected by your device's built-in security measures.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is generally no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about data collected by our ad partners, please refer to their respective privacy policies or contact us for guidance.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
