Frequently Asked Questions

How many puzzles are in MMMAth?

MMMAth contains 100 hand-crafted math puzzles across five categories: number sequences, algebra, shape equations, logic patterns, and mixed challenges.

Is the app free?

Yes. MMMAth is completely free to download and play. The app is supported by advertisements.

Do I need an account to play?

No. MMMAth does not require any sign-up or login. You can start solving puzzles immediately after downloading.

How do hints work?

Every puzzle has a hint button that reveals a clue to help you solve it. If you are still stuck after the first hint, an extra hint is available for a stronger nudge in the right direction.

Is my game progress saved?

Yes. Your progress is saved automatically on your device. However, if you uninstall the app or clear its data, your progress will be lost since it is stored locally.

Can I play offline?

The puzzles themselves work offline. However, ads require an internet connection, so you may see blank ad spaces when offline.

How do I remove ads?

There is currently no option to remove ads. We appreciate your support in keeping the app free for everyone.

What data does the app collect?

MMMAth itself does not collect any personal data. Third-party ad networks may collect device identifiers for ad serving purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

I found a bug or have a suggestion. How do I report it?

We would love to hear from you. Please send an email to the address below with a description of the issue or your suggestion. Include your device model and OS version if reporting a bug.
