MMMath - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

MMMath ("the app") is a math puzzle game. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how you can control or delete it.

Data We Collect Directly

MMMath does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers. The app does not require an account, login, or any personal information.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Game progress — highest unlocked level and completed levels
  • App preferences — any in-app settings you configure

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Advertising (AdMob)

MMMath uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is operated by Google LLC and may collect the following data to serve and measure ads:

Data typePurpose
Advertising ID (GAID)Advertising or marketing — to show relevant ads and measure ad performance
IP addressApproximate location for regional ad targeting
Device informationAd rendering and fraud prevention
App interaction dataAd performance measurement

This data is collected by Google AdMob, not by us. It is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

MMMath declares use of the Advertising ID for Advertising or marketing purposes as required by Google Play policy.

Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored app data (game progress, preferences) is retained on your device for as long as the app is installed.

To delete all your data:

  • Uninstall the app — permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • Clear app data — go to your device Settings → Apps → MMMath → Clear Data to reset progress without uninstalling.

For data collected by AdMob, you can reset or opt out of personalised ads via your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID or opt out of personalised ads.

We do not hold any of your data on our servers, so there is nothing to request deletion of from us directly.

No Accounts

MMMath does not require or support user accounts. No email address, name, or personal information is ever requested.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics SDKs. We do not track how you use the app or collect crash reports.

Children's Privacy

MMMath is a general-audience app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. AdMob may serve non-personalised ads to all users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
